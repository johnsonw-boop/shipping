Clarity First

Processing time vs delivery time

These are not the same thing. Processing time covers order review, packing, and handoff. Delivery time starts once the carrier has the shipment.

Stage What It Means Typical Timing
Processing Order verification, packing, labeling, and carrier handoff 0–1 business day
Transit Carrier movement from our warehouse to your shipping address 1–5 business days depending on region

Orders placed after the daily cutoff are processed on the next business day.

Support & FAQ

Common questions

Damaged or missing package?

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive as expected, contact us promptly so we can review the shipment and help resolve the issue.

  • Report shipping issues within 5 business days of delivery
  • Include your order number and any relevant photos
  • We will review the shipment and advise next steps quickly
When will my order ship?

Orders placed before 12 PM PST typically ship the same day if items are in stock.

Can I get faster shipping?

Yes. Expedited options can be offered for urgent orders depending on the destination.

Why does shipping cost vary?

Rates depend on destination, package weight, dimensions, and the service level selected.

Do you offer shipping outside Canada?

You can add USA shipping information here if that service is active in your store.

What happens if I enter the wrong address?

Contact us immediately. Address changes may be possible before the shipment is processed or scanned by the carrier.
