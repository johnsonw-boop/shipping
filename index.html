Common Questions
Damaged or missing package?

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive as expected, contact us promptly so we can review the shipment and help resolve the issue.

  • Report shipping issues within 5 business days of delivery
  • Include your order number and any relevant photos
  • We will review the shipment and advise next steps quickly
When will my order ship?

Orders placed before 12 PM PST typically ship the same day if items are in stock.

Can I get faster shipping?

Yes. Expedited options can be offered for urgent orders depending on the destination.

Why does shipping cost vary?

Rates depend on destination, package weight, dimensions, and the service level selected.

What is your return policy?

CECCORP handles refunds and returns on a case-by-case basis to ensure fair outcomes for each situation.

Refunds or replacements may be issued for non-delivered or incorrect orders (subject to verification), while delayed shipments may qualify for partial refunds at CECCORP's discretion.

Returns are typically accepted within 30 days if items are unopened and unused, though products showing misuse or improper storage may be declined.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method, with potential deductions if free shipping was included. Approved returns include a provided shipping label with processing times of 3-5 business days.

Custom and bulk orders may be non-refundable, and CECCORP reserves the right to determine the appropriate resolution in all cases. Contact support@ceccorp.ca.

Do you offer shipping outside Canada?

You can add USA shipping information here if that service is active in your store.

Do I need to pay duties or taxes, and how much will I pay?

Orders shipped to the United States may be subject to duties or tariffs, depending on customs rules. An estimated duties/tax amount is shown at checkout before you place your order.

What happens if I enter the wrong address?

Contact us immediately. Address changes may be possible before the shipment is processed or scanned by the carrier.
